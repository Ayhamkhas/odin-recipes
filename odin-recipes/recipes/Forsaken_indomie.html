Forsaken Indomie

Description

The Forsaken Indomie is a quick and easy recipe for a student or someone with small cooking experience. Overall, it's delicious and upgradable depending on personal preferences

Ingredients

  • 2 Indomie's packs
  • 200 gr of mushrooms
  • Peppers (by choice)
  • half an onion
  • Cream Sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cut your onion and peppers to small pieces and cook them on a pan with some butter on a medium fire
  2. When the onion starts browning add your mushrooms and the seasonings
  3. after couple of minutes add your cream milk and half of the cheese let it cook until becoming dense
  4. after cooking your noodles for 5 minutes in a boiling water, add it to the pan with the sauce
  5. As a final step you can add cheese on your plate and enjoy the meal
